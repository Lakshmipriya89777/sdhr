"Entrance to the paradise of Wisdom"

SDHR ( Sri Devapatla Harinadh Reddy) group of institutions laid the foundation to education in the year 1994 in Rayachoti, Kadapa District and extended its branches in Rayalaseema. The Main branch is located at the foot hills of Lord Venkateshwara, Tirupathi. We are elated to say "our College is the No.1 college" in delivering its quality education with in the two Telugu states.

Our college is laid on the motto to provide high standard education and to produce young potential candidates to excel in all spheres. We not only educate but train our students to reach heights in their career. Our college was established in the year 1994 with a major objective of providing high quality university education to everyone irrespective of their financial status. We started our journey initially with 64 students enrolled into B.Sc. Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and General B. Com in Kadapa, Rayachoti. But now we are not confined only to Kadapa but extended our branches to the other regions of Rayalaseema with Operating Branch located at the foot hills of Lord Venkateshwara, Tirupati. We are now offering more than eight graduate courses and more than five post graduate courses.

Our college is affiliated to A+ NAAC accredited SV University (Estd: 1974). Since then our students are excelling in all fields both in their education and in the Job market as well. It's our privilege to say that we are No. 1 in delivering quality education and producing professional students. We have a broad range of expertise in molding the students to achieve their desired goals and we want each and every student associated with our esteemed institution should go out as an employee. It's not even surprised to say that our students are settled in various high positions both in private and well as public sector with our guidance, training and support.
