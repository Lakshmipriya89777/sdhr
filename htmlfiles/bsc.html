About the Course

B.Sc Mathematics, Physics and Computer Science (B.Sc MPCs)

In this course curriculum includes Mathematics, Physics and computer Science as its major subjects along with languages and foundation courses. The students enrolled in this course are eligible for postgraduation in Mathematics, Physics or Computer science. Apart from that they can choose their career paths based on their areas of interest. But for these Students physics will be their major area where they can practice the theories and principles of physics and can experiment and co-relate them to the day to day situations and can go ahead with their experiments.

Duration

3 Years

Eligibility

Aspirants must have passed 10+2 with mathematics, Physics and Chemistry. The candidates should possess minimum 45% to 55% marks in aggregate and seat will be allotted on merit basis.

Fees

₹30,000 / year

B.Sc Biotechnology, Chemistry and Computer Applications (BCCA)

This course curriculum includes Bio technology, Chemistry and computer Applications as its major subjects along with languages and foundation courses. The students enrolled in this course are eligible for postgraduation in biotechnology, Chemistry or Computer science. Apart from that they can choose their career paths based on their areas of interest. But for these Students Bio technology and Chemistry will be their major areas where they deal with constituents of matter like atoms molecules, ions etc.; and its properties, structure, behavior, and interactions among them including numerous processes for the production of food and feed, pharmaceuticals, chemical products and energy sources which in turn helps in producing innovative techniques to diagnose, treat and prevent various diseases.

Duration

3 Years

Eligibility

spirants must have passed 10+2 with Botany, Zoology, Physics and Chemistry as their Subjects. The candidates should possess minimum 45% to 55% marks in aggregate and seat will be allotted on merit basis.
