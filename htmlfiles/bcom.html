About the Course

Bachelor of Commerce(Computer Applications)

This course is offered to the eligible candidates who want to become professionals in corporate management. It provides exposure to the candidates in the fields of commerce, business and administration. The students who have graduated in BCom are eligible for Masters in Business administration. The BCom graduates are eligible for varied jobs in administration, sales and marketing, Human resources, Finance etc., in both public as well as in private sectors based on their area of interests.

Duration

3 Years

Eligibility

Aspirants must have passed 10+2 from any stream with English as a subject with minimum 45% to 55% marks in aggregate and seat will be allotted on merit basis.

Fees

₹30,000 / year
