Campus Recruitment Training

SDHR providing free coaching-Campus Recruitment Training (CRT) to all students and is additional benefit from their academics to promote their campus placement selections. Apart from the basic field specific skill-set, the students are rising with ambition, aptitude, effective communication skills and team work abilities.

We have a special cell for Training and Placements to upskill the capabilities of our students. We have well expertise and experienced faculty to teach Short- cuts and concepts to ace in competitive exams. We provide free coaching for Quantitative aptitude, Reasoning ability, verbal ability, general knowledge, current affairs, spoken English, creative thinking, smart work, personality development, time management, Leadership skills, attitude and interview skills and a lot more.

We make our students to practice on the theory and concepts explained so that they will get familiar with concepts and practice them to get familiar with those concepts. We have a separate curriculum for the entire graduate course which is designed to enable our student's skills to compete in any competitive exams.

Apart from academics our students are entitled to have these competitive classes on daily basis. Our training is not limited to a single sector but we focus training on all possible sectors including private and public sectors viz., Software jobs, Marketing jobs, Banking Sector, Staff Selection Commission, Railways, Civil examinations, Group A-Group C.

We are also associated with Andhra Pradesh State Skill Development Centre (APSSDC) as soon as our students get admission into our college, they are entitled to enroll in APSSDC and they can use all the privileges of the Trainings and Drives that are conducted all over Andhra Pradesh with a separate Dash Board given to each and Every Student. It is up to the students to decide which sector to choose based on the career guidance provided by us.

We are here to show the possible ways and opportunities to have a successful outcome. Our only intention in our training is we want each and every student should be settled in one or the other job. We not only provide strong foundation in education which every college does but we are specialized in making them ready for their future employment.

This is the reason why most of the companies voluntarily approach us for campus placements and they are eagerly waiting for absorbing our wards into their companies.
